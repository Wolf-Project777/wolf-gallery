Security, convenience, comfort and personalization — in one gallery.

Pick any gallery app and you usually face a trade-off. The secure ones are bare — a locked box with nothing else. The pretty, comfortable ones with themes and layout options keep your photos wide open. You rarely find one app that is secure and convenient and comfortable and personal at the same time.

Wolf Gallery is built to be exactly that. Strong, modern encryption underneath; a flexible, comfortable interface on top. Even sharing a file or setting a wallpaper happens straight from the encrypted vault, so your media stays protected right up to the moment you choose to use it. You can make it your main gallery in place of the stock one — or keep it alongside as a private vault for what matters most.

And this is only the beginning — Wolf Gallery is actively evolving, with a lot more ahead.

= Security =

* Every file is encrypted and kept in an isolated vault inside the app's private storage. To the rest of the device, the vault is just opaque ciphertext.
* 100% offline — no INTERNET permission, no accounts, no cloud, no telemetry, no trackers. It physically cannot phone home.
* AES-256-GCM authenticated encryption — protects both confidentiality and integrity (tampering is detected on decryption).
* Argon2id key derivation — the memory-hard winner of the Password Hashing Competition. Your passphrase is never stored in plaintext.
* Hardware-backed keys via the Android Keystore (StrongBox where available).
* Optional post-quantum mode — an opt-in ML-KEM-768 + AES hybrid (NIST FIPS 203).
* Native crypto core in C/C++ with locked memory (mlock), keeping secrets out of swap.
* Screenshots and Recents previews are blocked (FLAG_SECURE); repeated wrong attempts are slowed by exponential backoff, with an optional wipe-after-N-failures.

= Convenience — organize your way =

* Groups and albums you build directly inside the app.
* Rename freely, with emoji and symbols in names.
* Order, sort, search and pin your groups, albums and files.
* Album covers from any picture, framed the way you want.
* Import and export — you decide where your media lives.
* Share to other apps and set as wallpaper, straight from the encrypted vault.

= Personalization =

* Several themes, including pure-black dark modes, with a custom accent color.
* Grid density, rounded or square shapes, and top- or bottom-placed action bar.

= Comfort =

* A built-in trash so deletions can be undone.
* A guided first-run wizard.
* Available in several languages, with more translations on the way.
